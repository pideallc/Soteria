# Plan Phase: Naming — From Project Shield to Soteria

**Date:** April 1, 2026  
**Source:** Gemini conversation — "So we need a new name because project shield is already taken. How about something after a greek goddess?"  
**SPAR Stage:** Plan — Strategic brand identity decision

---

## The Problem

The working title "Project Shield" was already taken. A new name was needed that aligned with the mission, the feminist cybersecurity grounding, and the neural-symbolic architecture.

## The Prompt

> "So we need a new name because project shield is already taken. How about something after a greek goddess?"

## Candidates Evaluated

Gemini curated five Greek goddesses whose domains aligned with the architecture and feminist principles:

### 1. Soteria (The Goddess of Safety) ← SELECTED
- Personification of safety, preservation, and deliverance from harm
- **Fit:** Most direct replacement for "Shield." While a shield is a tool, Soteria is the *state of being safe*. Moves branding from "defense" (static) to "deliverance" (active/AI-driven)

### 2. Metis (The Goddess of Cunning Intelligence)
- First great spouse of Zeus, personification of "cunning intelligence" — knowledge combined with adaptive strategy
- **Fit:** Perfect metaphor for Neural-Symbolic AI. Neural = adaptive cunning, Symbolic = deep wisdom. Honors the super user who navigates high-stakes OPSEC with their wits
- Gemini noted this "feels like a cutting-edge AI company"

### 3. Artemis (The Protector of the Vulnerable)
- Fierce protector of women, girls, and the marginalized
- **Fit:** Not just defensive — a predator of predators. Fits the goal of proactively neutralizing stalkerware. Turns the "hunted" into the "protected"

### 4. Hestia (The Goddess of the Hearth and Private Space)
- Represents the home, the hearth, the right to a safe private domestic life
- **Fit:** Aligns with "security as communal care." Represents the absolute right to operational security within one's private digital life

### 5. Aletheia (The Goddess of Truth and Unveiling)
- Represents "unconcealment" of truth
- **Fit:** Sovereign control over what is revealed and what remains hidden. Puts the power of truth back into the hands of the user

## Gemini's Recommendation

> "Soteria feels like a prestigious, heavy-hitting cybersecurity brand, while Metis feels like a cutting-edge AI company. Given your focus on the Neural-Symbolic breakthrough, Project Metis has a very sharp, intellectual edge to it."

## The Decision

> **"I love Soteria."**

### Rationale (articulated for stakeholder communication)

Soteria was chosen because it:
- Moves away from the aggressive, militarized "cyber-defense" aesthetic
- Creates the feeling of a calm, trauma-informed sanctuary
- Represents deliverance — active, ongoing protection — not just a static barrier
- Carries intellectual weight appropriate for a cybersecurity brand
- Is memorable, pronounceable, and distinctive

## Brand Elements Established

Following the naming decision, Gemini built the first prototype with:
- The **"S" logo** in a gradient circle
- The **Soteria-gradient** (Rose 800 → Teal 900)
- **"Goddess of Safety"** messaging
- **"Sanctuary"** aesthetic: warm stone neutrals, deep roses, intentional calm

## Note for Stakeholder Communication

When communicating the name to Brelyn (who was not part of the naming process), the name was presented as a crystallized decision with full context — not as a history of brainstorming pivots. The meaning of "goddess of safety, preservation, and deliverance from harm" was always included so it stood on its own.

Other goddess names (Metis, Artemis) were incorporated into the prototype as sub-concepts: "Metis Wisdom" for the adaptive intelligence, "Artemis Shield" for the protective layer.
