Privacy Engineering

The Blind Key Protocol

To protect you even from our own team, we use client-side cryptographic hashing. Your browser generates a unique SHA-256 fingerprint. The real data never leaves your device.

1

Client-Side Transformation

Your email is hashed in your browser using the Web Crypto API. The plaintext never touches our servers.

2

Irreversible One-Way Function

SHA-256 is a cryptographic hash — the original input cannot be reconstructed from the output. It is computationally infeasible to reverse.

3

Blind Communication

"We can communicate with you via the Key, but we never see the Person." Even a database breach reveals nothing.

Phase 1: Detection
